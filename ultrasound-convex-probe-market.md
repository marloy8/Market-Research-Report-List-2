<p><p>The Ultrasound Convex Probe market includes three types: Ultrasound Straight Probe, Ultrasound Angle Probe, and Ultrasound Curvature Probe. The Ultrasound Straight Probe is a linear probe that emits sound waves in a straight line, allowing for precise imaging of superficial structures. The Ultrasound Angle Probe emits sound waves at an angle, making it suitable for imaging structures that are difficult to visualize with a straight probe. The Ultrasound Curvature Probe is curved, enabling it to provide a wider field of view and better imaging of organs that are deep-seated or have complex shapes.</p></p>
