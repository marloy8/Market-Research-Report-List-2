<p><strong>The Nylon Knotted Fishing Net Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Depthway</li><li>Lengthway</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nylon knotted fishing nets can be categorized into two market types based on their manufacturing process: depthway and lengthway. Depthway nets are made by aligning and knotting the nylon threads vertically, creating a tightly meshed net. These nets are commonly used for catching smaller fish species close to the water surface. On the other hand, lengthway nets are made by aligning and knotting the nylon threads horizontally, forming a wider mesh. These nets are typically used for catching larger fish species at greater depths.</p></p>
