<p><strong>The CRISPR Gene Detection and Diagnostic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cutting Function Detection Method</li><li>Positioning Function Detection Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CRISPR gene detection and diagnostic market includes two types of methods for identifying genetic sequences. The cutting function detection method involves using CRISPR-Cas9 system to target specific genes and cut them, then analyzing the resulting DNA fragments to detect any mutations or variations. On the other hand, the positioning function detection method uses CRISPR-Cas9 to bind to specific gene sequences without cutting them, helping to precisely locate and identify specific genetic regions. These methods offer accurate and efficient detection of genetic markers for various applications in research, medicine, and agriculture.</p></p>
