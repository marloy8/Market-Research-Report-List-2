<p><p>Atopic dermatitis, also known as eczema, is a chronic skin condition characterized by inflammation, itching, and redness. <p><p>The atopic dermatitis treatment market consists of three main types: topical, oral, and injectable. Topical treatments are applied directly to the affected skin and include creams, ointments, and lotions. Oral treatments are taken by mouth and can include antihistamines, corticosteroids, and immune modulators. Injectable treatments involve injections directly into the skin or bloodstream and often include biologic medications. Each type of treatment aims to relieve symptoms such as itching, inflammation, and dryness associated with atopic dermatitis and improve the overall condition of the skin.</p></p>
