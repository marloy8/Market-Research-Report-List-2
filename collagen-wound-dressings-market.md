<p><p>Collagen wound dressings market types refer to the different sources of collagen used in wound dressings. Collagen can be sourced from bovine (cows), porcine (pigs), avian (birds), or other sources. Each source has unique properties and characteristics that make them suitable for specific wound types. Bovine collagen is widely used due to its high availability and strong collagen fibers. Porcine collagen is known for its similarity to human collagen and excellent biocompatibility. Avian collagen offers high purity and low antigenicity. Other sources may include marine or plant-derived collagen, providing alternative options for wound healing.</p></p>
