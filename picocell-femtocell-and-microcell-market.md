<p><p>Picocell, femtocell, and microcell are wireless communication systems used to enhance cellular network coverage and capacity. They all operate on lower power levels compared to traditional macrocell base stations, thus serving smaller areas with concentrated user demand.</p><p>Picocells are designed for large indoor environments such as shopping malls or office buildings. They cover an area ranging from tens to hundreds of meters and can support multiple users simultaneously. Femtocells, on the other hand, are intended for residential or small office use. They have a coverage area of about 10-50 meters and are ideal for improving network coverage in homes or small buildings. Microcells, also known as small cells, have a larger coverage area than femtocells but smaller than macrocells. They are commonly used in urban areas to offload network traffic and improve service quality.</p><p>The outlook for the picocell, femtocell, and microcell market is promising.
