<p><strong>The MRI Tomography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Field</li><li>Midfield</li><li>High Field</li><li>Ultra-High Field</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI Tomography System market can be classified into four types based on their magnetic field strength: Low Field, Midfield, High Field, and Ultra-High Field. Low Field systems have lower magnetic field strength, typically around 0.23-0.3 Tesla. Midfield systems have a slightly higher magnetic field strength of around 0.4-0.6 Tesla. High Field systems range from 1.5-3 Tesla, providing improved image quality and higher resolution. Ultra-High Field systems have the strongest magnetic field strength, usually above 3 Tesla, enabling even clearer images and better detection of abnormalities.</p></p>
