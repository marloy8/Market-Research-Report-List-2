<p><ul><li>Hall Based Current Sensor</li><li>Shunt Based Current Sensor</li><li>Other Magnetic Current Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The current sensor market for battery management systems (BMS) consists of three main types: Hall based current sensor, shunt based current sensor, and other magnetic current sensor. Hall based current sensors use the Hall effect to measure current and provide non-intrusive measurement capabilities. Shunt based current sensors utilize a low-resistance shunt resistor to measure current by detecting the voltage drop across the shunt. Other magnetic current sensors employ various magnetic sensing technologies to measure current. These sensors play a crucial role in accurately monitoring the current in BMS applications.</p></p>
