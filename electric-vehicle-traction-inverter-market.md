<p><p>An electric vehicle (EV) traction inverter is an essential component in an electric vehicle's powertrain system. The inverter also plays a crucial role in regenerative braking by converting AC power generated during braking back into DC power to be stored in the battery.</p><p>The global electric vehicle traction inverter market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.10%.
