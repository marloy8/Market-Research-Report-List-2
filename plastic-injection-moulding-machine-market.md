<p><p>A Plastic Injection Moulding Machine is a manufacturing equipment used to produce plastic products through the injection moulding process. This process involves melting plastic granules or pellets and injecting them into a mould cavity under high pressure. The molten plastic then cools and solidifies, taking the shape of the mould, resulting in the desired product.</p><p>The Plastic Injection Moulding Machine Market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. The rise in demand for plastic products across various industries, such as automotive, packaging, healthcare, and electronics, is driving the market growth. The ability of injection moulding machines to produce complex and intricate designs with high precision and efficiency has contributed to their rising adoption.</p><p>Additionally, the growing preference for lightweight plastic products to achieve fuel efficiency in automobiles and reduce packaging waste is further propelling market growth.
