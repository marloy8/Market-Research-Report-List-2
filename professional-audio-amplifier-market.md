<p><p>The professional audio amplifier market includes various types such as Class A, Class B, Class AB, Class G, and Class H. Class A amplifiers provide the best audio quality but are inefficient. Class B amplifiers are more efficient but suffer from distortion. Class AB amplifiers combine the advantages of Class A and B, providing good sound quality and efficiency. Class G amplifiers enhance efficiency by switching power supply levels. Class H amplifiers improve efficiency further by dynamically adjusting the power supply voltage based on the input signal.</p></p>
