<p><p>A medical call system, also known as a medical alert system or personal emergency response system, is a device that enables individuals to call for help in case of an emergency or medical condition. The market is primarily driven by the rising geriatric population globally, who are more susceptible to medical emergencies and require immediate medical attention.</p><p>The market growth is also fueled by the increasing demand for personalized and remote healthcare services. Technological advancements in the medical call system devices, such as GPS-enabled systems, fall detection sensors, and two-way communication features, have contributed to the market's growth. These advanced features enhance the overall effectiveness and efficiency of the medical call systems, thereby increasing their adoption among the elderly population.</p><p>Moreover, the growing awareness among individuals regarding the importance of healthcare management and safety is further driving the market growth.
