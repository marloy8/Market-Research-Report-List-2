<p><p>The market for dry rotary vane pumps includes two types: horizontal tank mount and vertical tank mount. Horizontal tank mount pumps are designed to be mounted horizontally on a tank or any other flat surface, making them suitable for installations with limited vertical space. On the other hand, vertical tank mount pumps are specifically designed to be mounted vertically on a tank, saving horizontal space while maximizing the use of vertical space. Both types are widely used in various industrial applications for their efficient and reliable pumping capabilities.</p></p>
