<p><p>Differential Pressure Sensor ICs are used to measure the difference in pressure between two points. These ICs are available in two market types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount refers to a method of mounting components directly onto the surface of a PCB, enabling smaller size and higher integration. On the other hand, Through Hole refers to a mounting technique that involves inserting leads of components into holes in a PCB, providing better mechanical stability but requiring larger space. Each market type caters to different PCB design and manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
