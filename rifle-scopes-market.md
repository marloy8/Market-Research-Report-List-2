<p><p>The rifle scopes market consists of various types of scopes that help shooters achieve better accuracy and precision while aiming. Telescopic sights are the most common type, providing magnification and crosshairs for distant targets. Collimating sights employ a red dot or holographic technology to display an illuminated reticle directly in the shooter's line of sight. Reflective sights use a partially reflective prism to display a reticle onto the shooter's vision. There are also other types of rifle scopes available in the market that offer unique features and functionalities to cater to different shooting preferences and conditions.</p></p>
