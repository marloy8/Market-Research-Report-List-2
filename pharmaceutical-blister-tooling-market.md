Seal tools are responsible for sealing the blister packs to ensure product integrity. Guide tracks/rails assist in the smooth movement of the blister packs during the packaging process. Perforation and scoring dies are used to create easy-to-open features on the blister packs. These tools play a vital role in the pharmaceutical industry by ensuring accurate and efficient blister packaging.</p></p>
