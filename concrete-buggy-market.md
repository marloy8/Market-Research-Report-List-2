<p><p>The concrete buggy market can be divided into two types: tracked and wheeled. Tracked concrete buggies are equipped with tracks similar to those used in construction equipment, providing improved traction and maneuverability in rough terrains or uneven surfaces. On the other hand, wheeled concrete buggies have conventional wheels, making them more suitable for flat and smooth surfaces. Both types serve the purpose of transporting and pouring concrete on construction sites, but their design and capabilities vary to cater to different working conditions and requirements.</p></p>
