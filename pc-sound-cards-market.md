<p><p>A PC sound card, also known as an audio card or an audio interface, is an expansion card or integrated circuit that enables a computer to produce and manipulate audio signals. The increasing demand for high-quality audio in gaming, entertainment, and multimedia applications is a key factor driving market growth. Firstly, there is a rising trend of manufacturers integrating sound cards directly onto motherboards, reducing the need for separate sound cards. This integration can result in cost savings for consumers and streamline the build process for manufacturers.</p><p>Another trend is the increasing demand for external sound cards or audio interfaces, particularly among professional musicians, producers, and content creators. External sound cards offer higher audio quality and more connectivity options than their internal counterparts, making them desirable for professionals who require precise sound reproduction.</p><p>In terms of market forecast, the PC sound cards market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.9% during the forecasted period.
