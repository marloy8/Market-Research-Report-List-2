<p><p>The low dielectric materials market can be categorized into three types: thermoplastic, thermoset, and ceramics. Thermoplastic materials are flexible and can be easily molded into various shapes when heated, solidifying upon cooling. Thermoset materials are rigid and cannot be reshaped once they are cured and hardened. Ceramics are non-metallic, inorganic materials that are highly resistant to heat and offer excellent electrical insulation properties. These different types of low dielectric materials cater to diverse applications and industries such as electronics, telecommunications, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
