<p><p>Molded pulp packaging is available in various types, including straw pulps, bamboo pulp, wood pulp, palm fiber, coconut fiber, and waste paper/carton board. Straw pulps are derived from agricultural waste, while bamboo pulp is obtained from bamboo plants. Wood pulp is sourced from trees, and palm fiber comes from the leaves of palm plants. Similarly, coconut fiber is extracted from the husk of coconuts, and waste paper/carton board is recycled from used paper or cartons. These different types of molded pulp packaging offer sustainable and eco-friendly solutions for various industries.</p></p>
