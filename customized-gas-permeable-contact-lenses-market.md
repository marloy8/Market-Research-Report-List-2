<p><strong>The Customized Gas Permeable Contact Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical</li><li>Toric</li><li>Multifocal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The customized gas permeable contact lenses market can be categorized into three types: spherical, toric, and multifocal lenses. Spherical lenses are designed to correct nearsightedness or farsightedness and have the same power throughout the lens surface. Toric lenses are specifically made to correct astigmatism, as they have different powers in different meridians of the lens. Multifocal lenses are meant for individuals with presbyopia, allowing them to see both near and far objects by incorporating multiple lens powers. These different types cater to specific vision correction needs of individuals.</p></p>
