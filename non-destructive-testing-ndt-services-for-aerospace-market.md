<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services are an integral part of the aerospace industry to detect defects and ensure the safety and reliability of aircraft components without causing damage. Visual inspections involve assessing the condition of components through visual means. Liquid Penetrant Testing uses dyes to detect surface defects. Acoustic Emission Testing detects defects through the release of stress waves. Leak Testing identifies leaks using various techniques. Radiography employs X-rays or gamma rays to detect internal flaws. Ultrasonic Testing uses high-frequency sound waves to identify flaws. Magnetic Particle Testing detects surface and near-surface defects using magnetic fields. Eddy-Current Testing assesses surface and subsurface defects using electrical currents. Other NDT methods include thermography, vibration analysis, and hardness testing.</p></p>
