<p><p>Microwave absorbers are materials designed to absorb and minimize the reflection and transmission of microwave energy. Pyramidal microwave absorbers consist of small pyramids, providing broadband absorption and high power handling capacity. Wedge microwave absorbers have a triangular shape, offering excellent reflection reduction and absorption in narrow frequency ranges. Walk on microwave absorbers are durable and can withstand foot traffic, making them ideal for anechoic chamber floors. Convoluted microwave absorbers have a convoluted surface structure, providing enhanced absorption in certain frequency bands. Hybrid microwave absorbers combine different absorber types for improved performance, while others refer to any additional absorbers not mentioned.</p></p>
