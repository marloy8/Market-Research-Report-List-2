<p><p>The shuttle rack market comprises various types of racks designed to efficiently store and retrieve goods. Stacking shuttle racks allow multiple shuttle carts to operate simultaneously on multiple levels, maximizing storage capacity. Mother-Child shuttle racks have a shuttle cart that carries a smaller shuttle within it, optimizing use of space. Multi-layer shuttle racks have multiple rack levels and shuttle systems, enhancing storage density. Four-direction shuttle racks enable shuttles to move in four directions, offering flexibility in accessing stored goods. Additionally, the market encompasses other innovative shuttle rack systems that provide unique features and benefits for different storage needs.</p></p>
