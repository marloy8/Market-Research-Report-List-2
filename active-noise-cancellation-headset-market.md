<p><p>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) headset market consists of three main types: Full Cup, On-Ear, and In-Ear. Full Cup headsets have large ear cups that fully enclose the ears, providing maximum noise isolation. On-Ear headsets rest on the ears, offering a balance between noise cancellation and comfort. In-Ear headsets are compact and fit directly into the ear canal, providing portability and decent noise reduction. Each type caters to different preferences and usage scenarios, allowing consumers to choose based on their desired level of noise cancellation and personal comfort.</p></p>
