<p><ul><li>Soft Copper Busbar</li><li>Laminated Busbar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the market of new energy vehicles, there are two types of busbars that are commonly used: soft copper busbars and laminated busbars. Soft copper busbars are made of copper material that is flexible and can easily be shaped. They provide efficient conduction of electricity while allowing for easy installation and maintenance. On the other hand, laminated busbars are made by stacking multiple layers of conductive and insulating materials, which offers advanced electrical performance, compact design, and improved heat dissipation. Both types of busbars play a crucial role in ensuring proper electrical connectivity and performance in new energy vehicles.</p></p>
