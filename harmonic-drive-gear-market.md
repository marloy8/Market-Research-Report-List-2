Cup style harmonic gears have a cup-shaped outer ring and are suitable for applications with high torque and compact design requirements. Hat style harmonic gears have a flange-shaped outer ring and offer high torque and low backlash. Pancake style harmonic gears have a flat design and are highly compact, making them ideal for space-constrained applications.</p></p>
