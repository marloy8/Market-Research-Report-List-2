<p><p>A Home Booster Pump is a device used to increase the water pressure in residential buildings. There are two main types of Home Booster Pump markets: Single Stage and Multiple Stages. </p><p>Single Stage pumps have a single impeller that helps in increasing the water pressure. These pumps are suitable for buildings with low to moderate water pressure requirements.</p><p>On the other hand, Multiple Stage pumps have multiple impellers arranged in series, which allows for a higher increase in water pressure. These pumps are ideal for buildings requiring higher water pressure, such as high-rise apartments or multi-story houses.</p></p>
