<p><ul><li>Regular Fibrous Casing</li><li>Peelable Fibrous Casing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fibrous casings are popular in the food industry for packaging sausages and other processed meats. Regular fibrous casing is a non-peelable option, meaning it cannot be removed before consumption. It is tightly sealed and provides excellent protection and shape retention during cooking. Peelable fibrous casing, on the other hand, has an additional layer within the casing that allows it to be easily peeled off after cooking, enhancing the convenience of eating. Both types offer durability, product safety, and contribute to an attractive appearance of the final product.</p></p>
