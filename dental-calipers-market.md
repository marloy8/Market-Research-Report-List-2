<p><p>Dental calipers are instruments used in dentistry for measuring various dental parameters. There are two main types of dental calipers: straight dental calipers and dial dental calipers. Straight dental calipers are manual instruments with two straight arms that can be used to measure different distances in the oral cavity. On the other hand, dial dental calipers have a dial gauge that provides a more precise and easy-to-read measurement. Both types of dental calipers are essential tools for dental professionals to accurately measure and assess dental structures and dimensions.</p></p>
