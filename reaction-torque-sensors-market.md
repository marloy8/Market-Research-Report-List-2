<p><p>Reaction torque sensors are used to measure the amount of torque applied to a rotating system. There are several types of sensors available, including Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors, Optical sensors, Strain Gauge sensors, and Magnetoelastic sensors. SAW sensors use the conversion of mechanical signals to electrical signals, while Optical sensors rely on light intensity changes to detect torque. Strain Gauge sensors measure the strain on a metal element when torque is applied, and Magnetoelastic sensors utilize magnetostrictive materials to detect torque. These sensors provide accurate and reliable measurements for various applications.</p></p>
