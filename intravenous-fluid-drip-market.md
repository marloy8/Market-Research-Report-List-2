<p><ul><li>Macro Drip</li><li>Micro Drip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intravenous fluid drip market is divided into two types: Macro Drip and Micro Drip. Macro Drip involves larger drops of fluids, allowing for a higher flow rate and is commonly used for patients requiring a larger volume of fluids. On the other hand, Micro Drip utilizes smaller drops, resulting in a slower flow rate, and is generally used for patients needing precise control over fluid administration. These distinctions enable healthcare professionals to choose the appropriate drip type based on the patient's specific requirements.</p></p>
