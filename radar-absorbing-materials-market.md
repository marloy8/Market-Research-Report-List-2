<p><ul><li>Magnetic</li><li>Dielectric</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radar Absorbing Materials (RAM) are used to minimize the radar reflection from objects, reducing their detectability. The market types of RAM include magnetic, dielectric, and hybrid materials. Magnetic RAMs consist of magnetically permeable particles that attenuate the radar waves through magnetic losses. Dielectric RAMs utilize properties of non-magnetic materials for absorbing the radar waves through dielectric losses. Hybrid RAMs combine both magnetic and dielectric properties to achieve enhanced absorption capabilities. These different types of RAM offer varying degrees of radar wave absorption, providing options for different applications and requirements in the market.</p></p>
