<p><p>The Universal Testing Machine (UTM) market consists of electromechanical and hydraulic types. Electromechanical UTM utilizes an electric motor to actuate the machine and performs tests by applying a load through a mechanical system. It is commonly used in industries where high precision is required. On the other hand, hydraulic UTM employs hydraulic power to generate force and carry out tests. This type is preferred for heavy-duty applications demanding high force capacity. Both types serve the purpose of conducting material testing, quality control, and research and development activities.</p></p>
