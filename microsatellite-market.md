<p><p>Microsatellites, also known as small satellites, are low Earth orbit (LEO) satellites that weigh less than 500 kg. They have become increasingly popular due to their versatility and affordability.</p><p>The Microsatellite Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The growth is fueled by the increasing demand for Earth observation applications, satellite communication services, and the need for space exploration. Additionally, the rising investment in space research and development activities by both governments and private companies is likely to propel market growth.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing trend of using constellations of microsatellites to provide global coverage and connectivity.
