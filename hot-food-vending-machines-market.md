<p><p>Hot food vending machines can be classified into two types: prefabricated heating type and onsite production type. The prefabricated heating type machines are designed to heat pre-packaged food items using built-in heating mechanisms. These machines require minimal maintenance and can be easily installed at various locations. On the other hand, onsite production type machines are equipped with cooking facilities to prepare food on the spot. These machines provide freshly cooked meals and allow for customization or menu changes as per customer preferences. They require regular maintenance due to the complexity of the cooking process.</p></p>
