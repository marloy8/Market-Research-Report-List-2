<p><strong>The Nonwoven Disposable Gloves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunlace/Spunbond</li><li>Wetlaid</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Needle Punched</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nonwoven disposable gloves market can be categorized into different types including Spunlace/Spunbond, Wetlaid, Airlaid, Needle Punched, and Others. Spunlace/Spunbond gloves are made by bonding fibers together using heat and pressure. Wetlaid gloves are manufactured by blending water, fibers, and binders to create a sheet-like structure. Airlaid gloves involve a process where fibers are dispersed in air and then bonded together. Needle Punched gloves are formed by mechanically interlocking fibers using barbed needles. The "Others" category includes gloves made by other methods like meltblown and chemical bonding.</p></p>
