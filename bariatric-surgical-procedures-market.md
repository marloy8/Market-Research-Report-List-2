<p><p>The Bariatric Surgical Procedures market includes several types of surgeries aimed at helping individuals with weight loss. Gastric Bypass Surgery involves creating a small stomach pouch and rerouting the digestive system. Adjustable Gastric Banding Surgery uses an adjustable band to restrict the size of the stomach. Gastric Sleeve Surgery involves removing a portion of the stomach to create a smaller stomach pouch. Duodenal Switch Surgery combines stomach reduction with rerouting of the small intestine. The "Others" market includes less common procedures like biliopancreatic diversion. These surgeries typically help patients achieve significant weight loss and improve their overall health.</p></p>
