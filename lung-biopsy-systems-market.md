<p><p>The lung biopsy systems market includes three main types: needle biopsy, thoracoscopic biopsy, and transbronchial biopsy. Needle biopsy involves inserting a thin needle into the lung to collect a tissue sample. Thoracoscopic biopsy involves using a flexible tube with a camera to visualize and take a tissue sample from the lung. Transbronchial biopsy involves passing a special instrument through the mouth or nose into the airways to collect lung tissue samples. These systems are essential for diagnosing lung diseases and determining appropriate treatment options.</p></p>
