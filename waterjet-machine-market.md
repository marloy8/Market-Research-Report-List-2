<p><p>The waterjet machine market can be divided into two types: high pressure and low pressure. </p><p>In the high pressure market, waterjet machines use intense water pressure to cut through materials like metal, stone, and composites. These machines operate at pressures above 60,000 pounds per square inch (psi), resulting in highly precise cuts and increased speed for industrial applications.</p><p>On the other hand, the low pressure market focuses on waterjet machines that operate at pressures below 60,000 psi. These machines are commonly used in applications such as food processing, cleaning, and certain precision cutting tasks that don't require extremely high pressures.</p></p>
