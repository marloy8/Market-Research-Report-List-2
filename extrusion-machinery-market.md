<p><ul><li>Single Screw Extrusion</li><li>Twin Screw Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Extrusion machinery is used in various industries for shaping and forming materials. Single screw extrusion refers to the type of machinery that utilizes a single rotating screw to melt and pump materials through a die to create a continuous profile. On the other hand, twin screw extrusion involves two intermeshing screws that work together to blend, mix, and transport materials. Single screw extrusion is commonly used for simpler applications, while twin screw extrusion is preferred for more complex tasks that require precise control and higher production rates.</p></p>
