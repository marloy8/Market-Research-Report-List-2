<p><p>Multiport plug valves are commonly used in various industries for controlling the flow of fluids. They are available in different types: manual, pneumatic, and electric. Manual multiport plug valves require manual operation, providing users with direct control over the valve. Pneumatic multiport plug valves are operated using compressed air, allowing for remote operation and automation. Electric multiport plug valves are powered by electricity and offer the advantages of automated control, ease of operation, and precise flow regulation. These different types cater to the diverse needs and preferences of industries and users.</p></p>
