<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The PC battery market has three main types: nickel-cadmium (Ni-Cd) battery, nickel-metal hydride (NiMH) battery, and lithium battery. The Ni-Cd battery utilizes a nickel electrode and a cadmium electrode, providing a good power output, but suffers from limited energy density and memory effect. NiMH battery improves on Ni-Cd with a higher energy density and no memory effect. Lastly, lithium battery offers even higher energy density, longer lifespan, and is lighter, making it the most popular choice due to its versatility and reliability in PC devices.</p></p>
