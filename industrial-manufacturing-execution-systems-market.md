<p><p>Industrial Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) are software solutions that help manufacturers plan, execute, and control their operations on the shop floor. On-premises MES refers to a system that is installed and maintained within the manufacturer's own infrastructure, providing complete control over the software and its data. On the other hand, on-demand MES, also known as cloud-based or Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) MES, is delivered over the internet, hosted by a third-party provider, allowing flexibility, scalability, and ease of access for manufacturers.</p></p>
