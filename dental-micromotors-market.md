<p><p>The dental micromotors market consists of two main types: portable micromotors and stationary micromotors. Portable micromotors are small, handheld devices that are designed for easy mobility and flexibility during dental procedures. They are lightweight and typically powered by rechargeable batteries, allowing dentists to move freely around the dental office. On the other hand, stationary micromotors are larger, more powerful motors that are fixed in a specific location. They are connected to a dental unit and provide a stable power source for more complex and demanding dental procedures. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of dental professionals.</p></p>
