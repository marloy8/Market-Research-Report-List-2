<p><p>Hydraulic clutch systems are of two types: Rotary Hydraulic Cylinder and Fixed-Hydraulic Cylinder. In the Rotary Hydraulic Cylinder market, the clutch assembly uses a rotary cylinder design, where the cylinder rotates during clutch operation, allowing for smooth engagement and disengagement of the clutch. On the other hand, the Fixed-Hydraulic Cylinder market utilizes a fixed cylinder design, where the cylinder remains stationary during clutch operation. This design is beneficial for applications that require a compact and space-saving clutch system.</p></p>
