<p><p>The dual-head laser marking machine market consists of three main types: CO2 laser marking machines, semiconductor laser marking machines, and fiber laser marking machines. CO2 laser marking machines use a carbon dioxide laser beam to engrave or mark various materials. Semiconductor laser marking machines utilize a semiconductor laser diode for precise and speedy marking on surfaces. Fiber laser marking machines employ a fiber laser beam for high-precision, permanent marking on different materials. These machines are widely used in industries such as electronics, automotive, and medical for product identification and traceability purposes.</p></p>
