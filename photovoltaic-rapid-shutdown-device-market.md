<p><p>Photovoltaic rapid shutdown devices are safety mechanisms used in solar energy systems to rapidly disconnect electrical connections during emergencies. These devices come in two main types: power optimizers and microinverters. Power optimizers increase energy harvest by providing maximum power point tracking and voltage optimization for each solar panel in a system. Microinverters, on the other hand, convert direct current (DC) from each solar panel into alternating current (AC) individually, improving overall system performance. Both types contribute to enhancing safety and efficiency in photovoltaic systems.</p></p>
