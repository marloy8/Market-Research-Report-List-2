<p><strong>The Automated Powder Flow Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Angle of Repose</li><li>Cylinder Test</li><li>Funnel Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Powder Flow Tester market consists of different types such as Angle of Repose, Cylinder Test, and Funnel Test. The Angle of Repose test measures the maximum angle at which a pile of powder remains stable, reflecting its flow properties. The Cylinder Test evaluates the powder's cohesion and flowability as it passes through a standardized cylinder. Lastly, the Funnel Test assesses the powder's flow rate by measuring the time it takes for a given amount of powder to flow through a funnel. These tests help industries determine the flow characteristics of powders for various applications.</p></p>
