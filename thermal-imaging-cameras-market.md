<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Thermal Imaging Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-wave</li><li>Mid-wave</li><li>Long-wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal imaging cameras are classified into three types based on the wavelength of their sensors: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave. Short-wave thermal cameras operate in the range of 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers, capturing high-resolution images with accurate temperature measurements. Mid-wave cameras work in the range of 3 to 5 micrometers and are effective in detecting objects with moderate temperature differences. Long-wave cameras operate in the range of 7 to 14 micrometers, allowing them to detect objects with low temperature variations efficiently. Different market segments utilize these types based on their specific requirements and application scenarios.</p></p>
