<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Alcohol free Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fermentation-limited Method</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcohol-free beer market can be categorized into two main types: fermentation-limited method and dealcoholization method. In the fermentation-limited method, the brewing process is carefully controlled to prevent the formation of alcohol while still producing a beer-like product. On the other hand, the dealcoholization method involves the removal of alcohol from regular beer through various techniques such as vacuum distillation or reverse osmosis. Both methods aim to provide consumers with a non-alcoholic beer option, catering to individuals who either cannot or choose not to consume alcohol.</p></p>
