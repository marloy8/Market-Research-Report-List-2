<p><p>The EUV mask-related inspection systems market consists of two main types: electron beam inspection systems and actinic systems for EUV mask inspection. Electron beam inspection systems use a high-energy electron beam to scan the surface of the mask and detect defects. Actinic systems for EUV mask inspection use an EUV light source that mimics the wavelength used in EUV lithography to inspect the mask for defects at the same wavelength. Both types of systems are crucial for ensuring the quality and integrity of EUV masks used in advanced semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
