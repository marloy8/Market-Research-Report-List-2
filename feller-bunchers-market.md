<p><p>Feller bunchers are machines used in forestry operations to cut and gather trees. There are two main market types for these machines: wheeled feller bunchers and tracked feller bunchers. Wheeled feller bunchers are equipped with wheels and are more suitable for flat or gently sloped terrain. They offer better mobility and speed but may have limitations in rough or muddy conditions. Tracked feller bunchers, on the other hand, have tracks instead of wheels, providing excellent traction and maneuverability in challenging terrains, such as steep slopes or muddy areas.</p></p>
