<p><p>Espresso coffee makers are available in three main market types: manually operated, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manually operated machines require the user's active involvement, from grinding and tamping the coffee to controlling the brewing process. Semi-automatic machines offer more convenience, as they automate some aspects, such as temperature control and pump operation, while still allowing users to control the extraction time. Fully automatic machines take automation further by automatically grinding, measuring, tamping, and brewing the coffee at the touch of a button, requiring minimal user intervention.</p></p>
