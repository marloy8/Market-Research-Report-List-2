<p><p>2-Ethylhexyl Methacrylate (2-EHMA) is a chemical compound widely used in various applications. The market for 2-EHMA is divided into two types: Esterification type and Type of Transesterification. In the Esterification type, 2-EHMA is produced by reacting 2-ethylhexanol with methacrylic acid. This method is commonly used for small-scale production. On the other hand, in the Type of Transesterification market, 2-EHMA is synthesized by reacting methacrylic acid with lower alcohols, such as methanol or ethanol. This process is more suitable for large-scale production due to its efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
