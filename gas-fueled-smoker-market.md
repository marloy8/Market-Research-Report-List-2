<p><strong>The Gas-Fueled Smoker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cabinet Smoker</li><li>Vertical or Cylinder Smoker</li><li>Offset Firebox Smoker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas-fueled smoker market consists of three main types: cabinet smokers, vertical or cylinder smokers, and offset firebox smokers. Cabinet smokers are designed with a tall, vertical chamber for uniform heat and smoke distribution, offering versatility in cooking different types of food. Vertical or cylinder smokers feature a rounded, vertical shape that optimizes smoke circulation and heat distribution, making them perfect for slow-cooking large quantities of food. Offset firebox smokers have a separate firebox connected to the smoking chamber, allowing for indirect heat and smoke control, making them ideal for smoking larger cuts of meat.</p></p>
