<p><p>Subsea production tree market comprises two main types: vertical subsea trees and horizontal subsea trees. Vertical subsea trees are used in offshore oil and gas wells where the tree is positioned vertically on top of the wellhead. They provide control and access to the well for maintenance and production purposes. Horizontal subsea trees, on the other hand, are designed for wells with horizontal flow paths. They offer similar functionalities as vertical trees but are specifically suited for horizontal wells, enhancing efficiency and productivity in these types of reservoirs.</p></p>
