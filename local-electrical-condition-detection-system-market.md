<p><strong>The Local Electrical Condition Detection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Online Detection</li><li>Offline Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Local Electrical Condition Detection System market is categorized into two types: Online Detection and Offline Detection. Online Detection refers to continuous monitoring of electrical conditions in real-time. It involves the use of sensors to detect abnormalities, failures, or fluctuations in the electrical system. This allows for immediate action to be taken to prevent any potential damage or loss. On the other hand, Offline Detection involves periodic inspections or testing of the electrical system to assess its condition when it is not operational. It helps identify potential issues and plan maintenance activities accordingly.</p></p>
