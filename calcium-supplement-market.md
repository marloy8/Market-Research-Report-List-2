<p><p>Calcium supplements are dietary supplements that provide the essential mineral calcium to the body. Calcium is important for maintaining strong bones and teeth, as well as for proper functioning of the heart, muscles, and nerves. Some people may require calcium supplements due to a deficiency in their diet or an increased need for calcium, such as during pregnancy or older age.</p><p>The global calcium supplement market has experienced significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 13.8% during the forecast period. The market is driven by factors such as increasing awareness about the importance of calcium for bone health, the growing aging population, and the rise in cases of osteoporosis and other bone-related disorders.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising prevalence of lactose intolerance and the growing popularity of vegan and dairy-free diets have also contributed to the demand for calcium supplements as alternative sources of calcium. Moreover, the increasing availability of calcium supplements in various forms, such as tablets, capsules, and chewable gummies, has made them more convenient and appealing to a wider consumer base.</p><p>In terms of trends, there has been a shift towards the use of natural and organic calcium supplements, as consumers are becoming more conscious about the ingredients and additives in their dietary supplements. Additionally, the market has witnessed the introduction of innovative calcium-infused products, such as calcium-fortified beverages and calcium-rich snacks, to cater to the changing preferences and lifestyles of consumers.</p><p>Overall, the calcium supplement market is poised for growth due to the increasing awareness about bone health, the rise in bone-related disorders, and the expanding consumer base seeking alternative sources of calcium.</p></p>
