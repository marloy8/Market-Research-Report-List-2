<p><p>A micro remote operated vehicle (ROV) is a small underwater robot that is remotely controlled and used for various underwater operations. Micro ROVs find applications in industries such as oil and gas, defense, maritime archaeology, research, and inspections of underwater structures.</p><p>The global market for micro ROVs has been experiencing significant growth over the past few years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. Manufacturers are working on integrating artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies into micro ROVs, enabling them to operate autonomously and perform complex tasks without constant human intervention.</p><p>Another trend is the miniaturization of micro ROVs. Manufacturers are focusing on reducing the size and weight of these vehicles while improving their performance and capabilities. This miniaturization allows for easier deployment and maneuverability in challenging underwater environments.</p><p>The market is also witnessing the adoption of advanced imaging and sensor technologies in micro ROVs. These vehicles are now equipped with high-resolution cameras, sonars, and other sensors to capture detailed underwater imagery and collect accurate data for inspection and surveying purposes.</p><p>Overall, the micro ROV market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years, driven by advancements in technology, increasing demand for underwater inspection and maintenance, and the development of autonomous capabilities.</p></p>
