<p><p>Formed in Place Foam Gaskets (FIPFG) is a method that allows for the production of gaskets directly on a surface, eliminating the need for separate sealing materials. The market includes three main types: liquid foam gaskets, which are applied in liquid form and then harden to create a seal; thixotropic foam gaskets, which have a semi-solid consistency and are ideal for vertical applications; and compact gaskets, which are solid and provide a tight seal for critical components. These gaskets cater to different sealing requirements and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
