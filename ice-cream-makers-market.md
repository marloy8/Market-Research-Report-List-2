<p><p>Ice Cream Makers Market Types:</p><p>Soft Ice Cream Makers are designed to produce a smoother and creamier ice cream texture. They incorporate air into the mixture during the freezing process, resulting in a lighter consistency. These machines are often used in commercial settings such as ice cream parlors and restaurants.</p><p>Hard Ice Cream Makers, on the other hand, produce a firmer and denser ice cream. They do not incorporate as much air during freezing, resulting in a thicker texture. These machines are commonly used in households and smaller-scale production settings where a more traditional ice cream consistency is desired.</p></p>
