<p><p>Micro displays are small-sized displays that are used in various applications such as wearable devices, headsets, and micro projectors. There are different types of micro displays available in the market. LCD displays use liquid crystal technology with backlighting for image projection. LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) displays utilize liquid crystals on top of a silicon chip. DLP (Digital Light Processing) displays use millions of tiny mirrors to reflect light onto a screen. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays emit light when an electric current passes through organic materials. Other types of micro displays include e-paper displays and AMOLED (Active Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays.</p></p>
