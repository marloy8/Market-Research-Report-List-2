<p><p>The laptop cooling pads market offers two main types: active cooling pads equipped with built-in fans and passive cooling pads without fans. Active cooling pads are designed with one or more fans, which circulate air to cool down the laptop. They provide efficient cooling but may generate some noise. On the other hand, passive cooling pads rely on heat-absorbing materials or raised surfaces to dissipate heat naturally. They do not require electricity or produce noise, making them a quieter and more energy-efficient option.</p></p>
