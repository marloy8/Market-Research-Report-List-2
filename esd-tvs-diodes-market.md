<p><strong>The ESD TVS Diodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Junction Capacitive Type</li><li>Low Junction Capacitive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ESD TVS diodes, also known as electrostatic discharge transient voltage suppressor diodes, are used to protect electronic devices from damage caused by electrostatic discharge. There are two types of ESD TVS diodes: high junction capacitive type and low junction capacitive type. </p><p>The high junction capacitive type has a higher junction capacitance, which allows it to provide better protection against ESD events. It is suitable for applications that require higher levels of ESD protection.</p><p>On the other hand, the low junction capacitive type has a lower junction capacitance, making it more suitable for applications with limited space or where high-speed signal transmission is required. It provides sufficient protection against ESD events while minimizing signal degradation.</p><p>Overall, the selection between these two types depends on the specific application requirements, such as the level of ESD protection needed and the space available for the diode.</p></p>
