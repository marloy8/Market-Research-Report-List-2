<p><p>The Infrared Fence market consists of two main types: Passive Infrared Fence and Active Infrared Fence. The Passive Infrared Fence uses sensors to detect changes in infrared energy caused by objects passing through the fence area. It is primarily used for security purposes in residential and commercial settings. On the other hand, the Active Infrared Fence emits infrared beams between transmitter and receiver units, triggering an alarm when the beam is interrupted. This type is commonly used in high-security environments such as military bases and prisons.</p></p>
