<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The E-paper Display (EPD) Chip market can be classified into two types: Discrete Controller and System on Chip (SoC). </p><p>Discrete Controller refers to a separate chip solely dedicated to controlling the EPD, independent of other functions. It offers high flexibility and customization options for device manufacturers.</p><p>On the other hand, System on Chip (SoC) integrates the EPD controller along with other essential components onto a single chip. This integration simplifies the design, reduces power consumption, and improves overall performance. SoC is popular in portable devices where space and power optimization are crucial.</p></p>
