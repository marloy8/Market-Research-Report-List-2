<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>RFID reader-writer market comprises four major types. LF (Low Frequency) RFID reader writers operate at a frequency range of 30-300 kHz, commonly used for access control and animal tracking. HF (High Frequency) RFID reader writers operate at 3-30 MHz and are employed in payment systems and transportation. UHF (Ultra High Frequency) RFID reader writers function at 300-960 MHz and are widely used in inventory management. MW (Microwave) RFID reader writers operate at frequencies above 1 GHz and are utilized in specialized applications such as asset tracking and toll collection.</p></p>
