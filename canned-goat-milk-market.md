<p><p>Canned goat milk offers two main market types: whole goat milk and skimmed goat milk. Whole goat milk is obtained by keeping fat content intact during the milking and packaging process. It provides a rich and creamy texture, preferred for its natural taste and nutritious properties. Both whole and skimmed goat milk cater to different preferences and dietary needs in the market.</p></p>
