<p><p>Organic protein powders are categorized into two main types: animal source proteins and vegetable source proteins. Animal source proteins are derived from animal products such as whey protein, collagen, and egg protein. These proteins offer a complete amino acid profile and are easily digestible. On the other hand, vegetable source proteins are obtained from plant-based ingredients like pea protein, hemp protein, and brown rice protein. These proteins are suitable for vegetarians and vegans and also provide a good amino acid profile. Both types of protein powders are popular among health-conscious individuals who seek organic and natural supplements for their fitness goals.</p></p>
