<p><p>ECG devices refer to medical devices used for monitoring the electrical activity of the heart. There are several types of ECG devices available in the market. The ECG resting system is used for recording the heart's electrical activity while the patient is at rest. The ECG Holter monitoring system enables continuous monitoring of the heart's activity over a longer period. The ECG stress testing system is used to assess the heart's response to exercise. Lastly, cardiopulmonary stress testing systems measure both cardiac and respiratory functions during exercise, providing comprehensive assessments of cardiovascular health.</p></p>
