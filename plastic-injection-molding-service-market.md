<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Plastic Injection Molding Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Molding</li><li>Insert Molding</li><li>Overmolding</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic injection molding service market comprises various types such as horizontal molding, insert molding, overmolding, and others. Horizontal molding involves the use of a horizontal machine that clamps the mold horizontally, offering high production capacity. Insert molding involves incorporating metal or other materials into the mold during the injection process, resulting in enhanced product durability. Overmolding involves the application of a second material onto a previously molded part to improve functionality or aesthetics. The "others" category encompasses any other type of plastic injection molding technique, which may include gas-assisted molding, multi-shot molding, or stack molding, among others.</p></p>
