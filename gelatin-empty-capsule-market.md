<p><p>The gelatin empty capsule market can be categorized into two types: hard gelatin capsules and soft gelatin capsules. Hard gelatin capsules are made of two pieces that fit together, providing a solid shell for powders or granules. They are commonly used for pharmaceuticals and dietary supplements. Soft gelatin capsules, on the other hand, have a flexible outer shell and are typically filled with liquid or semi-solid substances. Soft gelatin capsules are widely used for drugs, vitamins, and other health-related products. These two types cater to different requirements and applications in the market.</p></p>
