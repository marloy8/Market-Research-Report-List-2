<p><p>Rheumatic fever is a serious inflammatory condition that can develop as a result of inadequately treated strep throat or scarlet fever. It mainly affects children between the ages of 5 and 15. If left untreated, rheumatic fever can lead to severe damage to the heart valves, joints, and other organs.</p><p>The treatment for rheumatic fever involves various approaches to reduce inflammation, control symptoms, and prevent complications. Antibiotics, such as penicillin, are administered to eradicate the streptococcal bacteria causing the infection. Anti-inflammatory medications, such as aspirin or corticosteroids, are prescribed to reduce inflammation and relieve symptoms like joint pain and swelling. Additionally, in cases of severe heart damage, surgical interventions may be required, such as valve repair or replacement.</p><p>The Rheumatic Fever Treatment Market is expected to experience steady growth over the forecast period.
