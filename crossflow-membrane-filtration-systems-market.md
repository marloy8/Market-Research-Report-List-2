Microfiltration and ultrafiltration remove larger particles and macromolecules, nanofiltration is effective in removing smaller particles including divalent ions, reverse osmosis is utilized for desalination and removing dissolved solutes, while the "other" category includes specialized membranes for specific applications such as gas separation and electrodialysis.</p></p>
