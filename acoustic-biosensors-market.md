<p><strong>The Acoustic Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) Sensor</li><li>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for Acoustic Biosensors is comprised of two significant types: Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) Sensor and Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensor. The QCM Sensor operates by detecting and analyzing mass changes on the surface of a quartz crystal, providing accurate measurements for various applications. On the other hand, SAW Sensors work by transmitting acoustic waves through a sensor surface, detecting any changes in wave velocity caused by biomolecules binding. Both sensor technologies have gained prominence in the Acoustic Biosensors market due to their reliability, sensitivity, and extensive range of applications.</p></p>
