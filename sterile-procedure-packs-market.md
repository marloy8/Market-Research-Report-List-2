<p><ul><li>Single Use</li><li>Reusable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterile procedure packs market consists of two main types: single-use and reusable packs. Single-use packs are designed for one-time use and are disposed of after each procedure, ensuring sterility and preventing cross-contamination. Reusable packs, on the other hand, are sterilized and can be used multiple times before undergoing another sterilization process. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages. Single-use packs provide convenience, reduce the risk of infection, but can be more expensive. Reusable packs offer cost savings in the long run but require proper cleaning, sterilization, and maintenance procedures to ensure safety and effectiveness.</p></p>
