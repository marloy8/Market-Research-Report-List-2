<p><p>Hot melt adhesive dispensing equipment is used to apply hot melt adhesives in various industries such as packaging, automotive, electronics, and construction. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for hot melt adhesives in various industries. Hot melt adhesives offer advantages such as fast curing time, strong bonding strength, and resistance to heat and moisture, which are driving their adoption across industries. The growth in packaging industry, particularly e-commerce and food & beverage packaging, is also contributing to the demand for hot melt adhesive dispensing equipment.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology and the development of efficient dispensing equipment are further boosting the market growth. The automation of dispensing processes is also driving the demand for advanced hot melt adhesive dispensing equipment.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a rising preference for electric dispensing equipment over pneumatic systems. The integration of Industry 4.0 technologies, such as IoT and AI, in hot melt adhesive dispensing equipment is another emerging trend in the market.</p><p>Overall, the hot melt adhesive dispensing equipment market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for hot melt adhesives and the introduction of innovative and efficient dispensing equipment.</p></p>
