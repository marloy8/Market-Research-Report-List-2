<p><p>The vascular snare market finds significant application in the treatment of inferior vena cava (IVC) disorders. Vascular snares are used to capture and remove foreign bodies, blood clots, or devices from the IVC, restoring normal blood flow. Apart from IVC, vascular snares are also utilized in other markets related to vascular interventions, such as the removal of emboli or foreign bodies from the arteries.</p></p>
