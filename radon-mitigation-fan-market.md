<p><p>Radon mitigation fans are classified into three types based on their suction power: low suction, moderate suction, and high suction. Low suction fans are designed for areas with lower levels of radon concentration. They are suitable for homes where radon levels are not significantly high and require minimal suction power. Moderate suction fans are used in places with moderate levels of radon, offering a higher suction power. High suction fans, on the other hand, are employed for areas with high radon concentration, requiring substantial suction to effectively reduce radon levels.</p></p>
