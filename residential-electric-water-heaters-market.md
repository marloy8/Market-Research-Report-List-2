<p><p>Residential Electric Water Heaters market comprises two major types: Tankless Electric Water Heaters and Tank Electric Water Heaters. Tankless Electric Water Heaters are designed to heat water on-demand as it flows through the unit, eliminating the need for a storage tank and providing continuous hot water supply with greater energy efficiency. On the other hand, Tank Electric Water Heaters store and heat a specific amount of water in a tank, delivering hot water whenever needed but with less energy efficiency compared to tankless models.</p></p>
