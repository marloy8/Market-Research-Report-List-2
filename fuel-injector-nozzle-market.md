<p><p>The Fuel Injector Nozzle Market consists of several types, namely SFI (Sequential Fuel Injection), GDI (Gasoline Direct Injection), SFI+GDI (Sequential Fuel Injection and Gasoline Direct Injection), and DDI (Direct Diesel Injection). SFI injects fuel in a continuous and sequential manner, whereas GDI directly injects fuel into the combustion chamber. SFI+GDI combines the benefits of both systems, optimizing fuel efficiency and power output. DDI refers to direct injection of diesel fuel into the combustion chamber. These various types cater to different engine configurations and aim to enhance fuel economy, performance, and emission control.</p></p>
