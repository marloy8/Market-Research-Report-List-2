<p><strong>The Antihypertensive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diuretics</li><li>Angiotensin Converting Enzyme (ACE) Inhibitors</li><li>Angiotensin Receptor Blockers (ARBs)</li><li>Calcium Channel Blockers</li><li>Beta Blockers</li><li>Alpha Blockers</li><li>Vasodilators & Renin Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antihypertensive market is categorized into various types. Diuretics help the body eliminate salt and water to lower blood pressure. ACE inhibitors relax blood vessels, while ARBs block a hormone responsible for narrowing blood vessels. Calcium channel blockers hinder calcium from entering the heart and blood vessel walls, reducing blood pressure. Beta blockers reduce heart rate and force of contraction, while alpha blockers relax certain muscles, allowing blood to flow more easily. Vasodilators widen blood vessels, and renin inhibitors reduce the production of a chemical that causes blood vessels to narrow.</p></p>
