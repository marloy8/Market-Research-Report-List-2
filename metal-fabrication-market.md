<p><p>Metal fabrication is a diverse market that encompasses various processes. Metal welding involves joining pieces of metal together using heat or pressure. Metal machining involves shaping metal through cutting, drilling, or grinding. Metal forming refers to techniques used to shape metal into desired forms through bending, stretching, or hammering. Metal shearing involves cutting metal sheets into smaller sizes. Metal cutting involves slicing metal using saws, lasers, or plasma torches. Metal folding involves bending metal sheets along straight lines. Metal rolling forms metal into various shapes using rollers. Metal stamping is a process of creating shapes or patterns on metal by pressing a die into it. Metal punching involves creating holes or indentations in metal using a punch and die mechanism.</p></p>
