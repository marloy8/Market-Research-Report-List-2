<p><p>Wire mesh decking is a popular choice for storage systems due to its durability and versatility. There are two main types in the market: flared channel decking and U-channel decking. Flared channel decking features flared supports on each side, allowing for easy installation and removal. On the other hand, U-channel decking has U-shaped supports, making it more stable and suitable for heavier loads. Both types provide efficient airflow, visibility, and support for storing items on pallet racks.</p></p>
