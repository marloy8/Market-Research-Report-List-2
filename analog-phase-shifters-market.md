The increasing demand for phased array radars and communication systems is one of the key factors driving market growth. Phased array radars are widely used in military applications for long-range detection and tracking. Analog phase shifters are utilized in communication systems to ensure accurate phase alignment and beam steering in antennas, leading to improved signal quality and coverage.
