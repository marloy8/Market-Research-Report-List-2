<p><p>Optic Nerve Disorders Treatment refers to the various therapeutic interventions aimed at managing and treating disorders related to the optic nerve. Medications, such as corticosteroids and immunosuppressive drugs, may be prescribed to reduce inflammation and manage certain conditions like optic neuritis. Surgical interventions may be required in cases of optic nerve compression or to address underlying causes like tumors or blood vessel abnormalities.
