<p><p>The automatic capacitor banks market consists of three main types: LV (low voltage), MV (medium voltage), and HV (high voltage) capacitor banks. LV capacitor banks are designed for low voltage applications, typically below 1 kV. MV capacitor banks are used in medium voltage systems, ranging from 1 kV to 72.5 kV. HV capacitor banks are built for high voltage applications, typically above 72.5 kV. Each type of capacitor bank is utilized in various sectors to improve power factor and mitigate voltage fluctuations based on the specific voltage requirements.</p></p>
