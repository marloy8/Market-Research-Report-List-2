<p><p>Construction management systems are software platforms used by construction companies to streamline and manage their projects. There are two main types of construction management system markets: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise systems require companies to host the software on their own servers, providing complete control over the system but requiring higher upfront costs. Cloud-based systems, on the other hand, are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. They offer scalability, flexibility, and lower costs but require a reliable internet connection. Both types have their own advantages and considerations depending on the company's needs and resources.</p></p>
