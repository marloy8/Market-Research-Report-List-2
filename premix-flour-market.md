<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The premix flour market consists of three main types: complete mix, dough-base mix, and dough concentrates. Complete mix refers to a ready-to-use blend that only requires the addition of water and other ingredients to create various bakery products. Dough-base mix is a versatile blend that requires the addition of yeast, water, and other ingredients to make dough. Lastly, dough concentrates are specialized blends that require the addition of flour, yeast, and water to create dough for specific products. These premix flour types offer convenience and ensure consistent quality in bakery production.</p></p>
