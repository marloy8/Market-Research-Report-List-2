<p><strong>The Fiber Laser Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DFB Laser Chip</li><li>VCSEL Chip</li><li>EML Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber laser chip market comprises three main types of chips: DFB laser chips, VCSEL chips, and EML chips. DFB laser chips, or Distributed Feedback laser chips, emit coherent light and are widely used in communication systems due to their narrow linewidth and high spectral purity. VCSEL chips, or Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Laser chips, produce lower-power and low-cost lasers suitable for applications like optical sensors, data communication, and computer networks. EML chips, or Electro-absorption Modulated Laser chips, combine a DFB laser with an electro-absorption modulator to enable direct modulation for high-speed data transmission and broadcasting applications.</p></p>
