<p><p>The floor mounted air conditioner market can be categorized into two main types: conventional and large type. Conventional floor mounted air conditioners are designed for small to medium-sized spaces and are typically installed closer to the ground. They provide effective cooling or heating by blowing air at a low height. On the other hand, large type floor mounted air conditioners are specifically designed for spacious areas and have a higher capacity for cooling or heating. They are capable of delivering strong airflow over longer distances and are suitable for large rooms or open floor plans.</p></p>
