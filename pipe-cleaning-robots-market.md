<p><p>The market for pipe cleaning robots can be categorized into two types: pressure-based cleaning robots and tool-based cleaning robots. Pressure-based cleaning robots use high-pressure water jets to clean pipes, effectively removing dirt, debris, and blockages. On the other hand, tool-based cleaning robots employ mechanical tools, such as brushes or cutters, to physically remove the buildup inside pipes. Both types of robots offer efficient and thorough cleaning solutions, catering to different pipe cleaning requirements in various industries and settings.</p></p>
