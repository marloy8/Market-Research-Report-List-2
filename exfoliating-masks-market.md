<p><p>Exfoliating masks refer to skincare products designed to remove dead skin cells and impurities, promoting a smoother complexion. Patch masks are typically single-use sheets, infused with exfoliating ingredients, such as fruit acids or enzymes, that adhere to the face for a specified period. They provide targeted treatment and are convenient to apply. Smear masks, on the other hand, are gel, cream, or clay-based masks that are spread directly on the face. They also contain exfoliating agents and are left on the skin for a specific time before being washed off, providing a more overall treatment.</p></p>
