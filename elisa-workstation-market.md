<p><ul><li>Pipetting System</li><li>Washer</li><li>Shaker</li><li>Incubator</li><li>Buffers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ELISA Workstation market includes various types of equipment used in ELISA testing. Pipetting systems are automated instruments that accurately measure and transfer samples and reagents. Washers are used to remove unbound substances from the ELISA plate. Shakers ensure proper mixing and distribution of samples and reagents. Incubators provide a controlled environment for the ELISA plates to facilitate the binding of molecules. Buffers are solutions used to maintain optimal conditions during the ELISA process. These different types of equipment play crucial roles in performing efficient and accurate ELISA tests.</p></p>
