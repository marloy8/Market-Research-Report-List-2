<p><p>The Automotive Lane Departure Warning System market can be categorized into three types - video sensors, laser sensors, and infrared sensors. Video sensors use cameras to detect lane markings and monitor the vehicle's position on the road. Laser sensors employ lasers to measure the distance between the vehicle and the road markings. Infrared sensors utilize infrared light to detect lane boundaries. These different sensor types help in warning drivers if they unintentionally drift out of their lane, ensuring safer driving and reducing the risk of accidents.</p></p>
