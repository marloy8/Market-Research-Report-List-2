<p><strong>The Parasitology Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microscopic Identification</li><li>Molecular Techniques</li><li>Immunological Techniques</li><li>Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parasitology testing includes various techniques for identifying and diagnosing parasitic infections. Microscopic identification involves examining samples under a microscope to identify parasites based on their morphology. Molecular techniques use DNA analysis to detect and identify parasites more accurately. Immunological techniques detect specific antibodies or antigens produced by the body in response to parasites. Rapid diagnostic tests (RDTs) provide quick results by detecting parasite antigens or antibodies within a short time. These different types of testing methods play a crucial role in diagnosing and managing parasitic infections.</p></p>
