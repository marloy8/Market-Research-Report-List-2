<p><p>Bilge pumps are devices designed to remove water that accumulates in the bilge of a boat or ship. There are two main types of bilge pumps available in the market: electric bilge pumps and manual bilge pumps. Electric bilge pumps are powered by electricity and automatically remove water from the bilge when the water reaches a certain level. Manual bilge pumps, on the other hand, require manual operation to remove the water. Both types serve the purpose of keeping the bilge area dry and free from water accumulation to ensure the safety and proper functioning of the vessel.</p></p>
