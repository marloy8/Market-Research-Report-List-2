<p><p>The lighter market can be divided into two main types: gas lighters and liquid lighters. Gas lighters, also known as butane lighters, use a pressurized gas and a spark mechanism to produce a flame. They are easy to use, portable, and commonly used for lighting cigarettes or candles. On the other hand, liquid lighters, often referred to as petrol lighters, utilize a flammable liquid fuel, such as naphtha or gasoline, to generate a flame. They require regular refilling and maintenance but are popular for their durability and functionality.</p></p>
