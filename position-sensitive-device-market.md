<p><p>Position sensitive devices (PSDs) are sensors used to determine the precise location of an object or detect movement. One-dimensional PSDs measure the position of an object along a single axis, such as detecting the linear motion of a slider. Two-dimensional PSDs, on the other hand, can track the position of an object in both the x and y directions, making them suitable for applications such as touchscreen displays or optical encoders. Both types of PSDs are essential in various industries, including robotics, automotive, and consumer electronics, enabling accurate position detection and control.</p></p>
