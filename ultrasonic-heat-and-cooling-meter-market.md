<p><p>The Ultrasonic Heat and Cooling Meter market can be categorized into two types - Integrated Heat and Cooling Meter and Split Heat and Cooling Meter. </p><p>Integrated Heat and Cooling Meters are built as a single unit to simultaneously measure and analyze both heat and cooling consumption in a particular space. This type of meter provides a comprehensive understanding of the overall energy usage for thermal regulation in a building.</p><p>On the other hand, Split Heat and Cooling Meters are designed to separately measure and analyze heat and cooling consumption. This allows for more precise and independent monitoring of energy usage for heating and cooling purposes in different areas or units within a building.</p></p>
