<p><strong>The Bowed Dulcimer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>All Solid Wood</li><li>Laminated Wood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bowed dulcimer market can be categorized into two types: All Solid Wood and Laminated Wood. All Solid Wood dulcimers are constructed entirely from solid pieces of wood, which provides a rich and resonant tone. These instruments tend to be pricier due to the high-quality materials used. On the other hand, Laminated Wood dulcimers are made by layering thin sheets of wood together. While less expensive, they may not produce the same level of tonal complexity as their All Solid Wood counterparts.</p></p>
