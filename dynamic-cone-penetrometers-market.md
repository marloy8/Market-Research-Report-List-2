<p><p>Dynamic Cone Penetrometers (DCP) are commonly used geotechnical tools to measure soil density and bearing capacity. There are two main market types: Single-Mass DCP and Dual-Mass DCP. Single-Mass DCP features a single mass that hits the anvil, causing soil penetration, while Dual-Mass DCP utilizes two masses, providing enhanced energy transfer and more accurate readings. These instruments are widely utilized in construction, road building, and civil engineering projects to assess soil strength, compaction, and stability, aiding in the design and execution of safe and durable structures.</p></p>
