<p><p>The Transient Suppressor (TVS) Diodes Market includes two main types: Uni-Polar TVS and Bi-Polar TVS. Uni-Polar TVS diodes provide protection against voltage spikes in one direction only, making them suitable for applications where voltage surges occur in a single polarity. Bi-Polar TVS diodes offer protection against voltage spikes in both positive and negative directions, making them suitable for applications where voltage surges can occur in either polarity. Both types are used to divert excessive electrical energy away from sensitive electronic components, ensuring their protection from damage caused by transient voltage spikes.</p></p>
