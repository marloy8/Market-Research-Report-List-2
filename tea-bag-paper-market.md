<p><p>The tea bag paper market can be divided into two main types: heat-sealable tea bag paper and non-heat-sealable tea bag paper. Heat-sealable tea bag paper is designed to be sealed using heat, creating an airtight seal for the tea bags. It provides excellent barrier properties to maintain the freshness and aroma of the tea. On the other hand, non-heat-sealable tea bag paper does not require heat for sealing and is often used for tea bags that are individually wrapped or sealed using other methods. Both types cater to different packaging needs and preferences in the tea industry.</p></p>
