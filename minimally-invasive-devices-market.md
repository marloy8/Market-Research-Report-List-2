<p><p>The minimally invasive devices market consists of three main types: handheld instruments, guiding devices, and inflation systems. Handheld instruments are small tools that are used to perform procedures inside the body through small incisions. Guiding devices, on the other hand, provide guidance and navigation during minimally invasive surgeries. These devices help the surgeon to accurately place and maneuver other instruments. Lastly, inflation systems are used to inflate organs or body cavities with gas or fluids during surgeries, creating more space for the surgeon to operate.</p></p>
