Class 1 CIS indicates only one parameter, such as temperature, while Class 4 CIS verifies parameters like time, temperature, and presence of steam. Class 5 CIS provides the functionality of Class 4 plus detecting the presence of hydrogen peroxide or peracetic acid. Class 6 CIS monitors additional parameters, including ethylene oxide.
