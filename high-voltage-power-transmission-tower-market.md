<p><p>The High Voltage Power Transmission Tower market can be categorized into two types - self-standing towers and cable towers. Self-standing towers are designed to support power transmission lines independently without any additional support structures. These towers are commonly used in areas with stable soil conditions. On the other hand, cable towers require additional support from guy ropes or cables to maintain stability. This type of tower is mainly used in areas with unstable soil or in situations where there is a need to span long distances between tower locations.</p></p>
