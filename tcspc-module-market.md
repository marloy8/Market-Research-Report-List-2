<p><strong>The TCSPC Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single channel</li><li>Multi-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TCSPC (Time-Correlated Single Photon Counting) module market can be categorized into two types: single-channel and multi-channel. In the single-channel market, TCSPC modules are designed to measure the timing information of a single photon detection event. They are used in applications where precise timing resolution is crucial, such as fluorescence lifetime imaging microscopy (FLIM). On the other hand, multi-channel TCSPC modules have the ability to process the timing information of multiple photon detection events simultaneously, making them suitable for applications that require high-speed and parallel data acquisition, such as time-resolved spectroscopy or photon correlation experiments.</p></p>
