<p><p>Distribution meter market types can be categorized into two main segments: outdoor use and indoor use. Outdoor use distribution meters are specifically designed to withstand harsh environmental conditions such as extreme temperatures, humidity, and exposure to sunlight. These meters are usually installed on utility poles, streetlights, or other outdoor infrastructure for accurate measurement and monitoring of electrical distribution. On the other hand, indoor use distribution meters are intended for installation inside buildings or substations, providing accurate monitoring and measurement of electricity consumption within these enclosed spaces.</p></p>
