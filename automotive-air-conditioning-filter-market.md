<p><ul><li>Particle filter</li><li>Activated carbon filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air conditioning filter market includes two main types: particle filters and activated carbon filters. Particle filters are designed to trap and remove dust, pollen, and other small particles from the air entering the vehicle's cabin, preventing them from circulating inside. Activated carbon filters, on the other hand, not only capture particles but also absorb odors and harmful gases, enhancing the overall air quality within the cabin. These filters are essential in ensuring a clean and comfortable environment for passengers while driving.</p></p>
