<p><p>Thermal IR sensors utilize infrared radiation to detect and measure temperature variations. There are two main types of sensors in this market: photon detection and thermal detection. Photon detection sensors, such as photodiodes or phototransistors, detect the energy emitted by photons in the infrared range. On the other hand, thermal detection sensors, such as microbolometers or thermopiles, directly measure the heat emitted by the objects. Both types of sensors are integral in applications like thermal imaging, firefighting, healthcare, and security systems.</p></p>
