<p><p>The marine weather forecast market consists of three main types: short-range, medium-range, and long-range forecasting. Short-range forecasting focuses on predicting weather conditions within the next 24 to 48 hours. Medium-range forecasting extends the prediction horizon to around 3-7 days. Long-range forecasting, on the other hand, covers forecasts beyond the 7-day mark and can provide insights into climate patterns and trend analysis. These different market types cater to the varying needs of industries such as shipping, fishing, offshore operations, and leisure boating, enabling them to make informed decisions and mitigate potential risks.</p></p>
