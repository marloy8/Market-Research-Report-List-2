<p><p>Dual Channel Digital Isolators market refers to a class of electronic devices that provide galvanic isolation between two separate circuits. Capacitive Coupling isolators use a capacitive element to transmit signals across an isolation barrier, providing low power consumption and high data rates. Magnetic Coupling isolators utilize magnetic fields to transmit signals, offering superior noise immunity and isolation performance. Giant Magnetoresistive GMR isolators employ GMR effect to achieve high-speed digital signal isolation. Each of these technologies has its own benefits and applications, catering to different industry requirements.</p></p>
