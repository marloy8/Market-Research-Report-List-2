<p><p>Unified Communications Management is a market that offers two distinct types: Single Tenant and Multi Tenant. In the Single Tenant model, businesses have dedicated infrastructure and software exclusively for their own use. This allows for greater customization and control, but it can come at a higher cost. On the other hand, the Multi Tenant model offers a shared infrastructure and software among multiple businesses. This results in lower costs and easier scalability, but customization may be limited. Companies can choose between these options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
