<p><p>The smartphone touch screen market consists of various types, including capacitive technology, resistive technology, and others. Capacitive technology screens are responsive to human touch and utilize conductive materials to detect changes in the screen's electrical field. Resistive technology screens, on the other hand, rely on pressure to register touch, using two conductive layers that come in contact when pressed. Other types include optical touch screens, which use infrared light and sensors, and acoustic touch screens, which detect sound waves.</p></p>
