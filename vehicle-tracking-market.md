<p><p>The vehicle tracking market can be classified into two types: wired tracking and wireless tracking. Wired tracking involves the installation of physical devices and sensors in vehicles, connected through wires to a central tracking system. This type of tracking is robust and provides accurate real-time data. On the other hand, wireless tracking relies on GPS and cellular technologies to transmit vehicle information wirelessly to the tracking system. It offers flexibility in installation and scalability, making it suitable for a wide range of vehicles. Both types have their distinct advantages and can cater to different tracking requirements.</p></p>
