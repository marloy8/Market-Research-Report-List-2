<p><p>The Dry Sorbent Injection (DSI) system is used in industries to control air pollution, particularly emissions of sulfur dioxide (SO2) and other acid gases. There are two main types of DSI systems: Sodium Bicarbonate Based and Hydrated Lime Based. Sodium Bicarbonate Based DSI systems use sodium bicarbonate, a chemical compound, to neutralize and remove the acid gases. On the other hand, Hydrated Lime Based DSI systems use hydrated lime, a fine white powder derived from limestone, to remove the acid gases through a chemical reaction. Both types of DSI systems offer effective solutions for reducing air pollution.</p></p>
