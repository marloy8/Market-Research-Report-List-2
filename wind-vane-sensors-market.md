<p><p>Wind vane sensors are devices used to measure wind direction. There are two types of wind vane sensors available in the market: wired type and wireless type. The wired type requires a physical connection between the sensor and the measuring equipment, allowing for real-time data transmission. On the other hand, the wireless type utilizes wireless technology such as radio waves or Bluetooth to transmit data from the sensor to the measuring equipment. This enables more flexibility in terms of installation and eliminates the need for physical connections.</p></p>
