<p><p>The biotechnology separation systems market is categorized into different types, including membrane filtration, liquid chromatography, centrifuge, electrophoresis, flow cytometry, and others. Membrane filtration involves the use of a thin barrier to separate particles based on their size. Liquid chromatography uses a mobile phase and a stationary phase to separate molecules based on their affinity for the two phases. Centrifuge separates particles by their density using rapid spinning. Electrophoresis separates molecules in an electric field based on their charge and size. Flow cytometry analyzes and sorts cells or particles based on their fluorescence and scattering properties.</p></p>
