<p><p>The electronic load market consists of three main types: benchtop, system, and module. </p><p>Benchtop electronic loads are standalone devices used for testing and characterizing various electronic components and devices. They are usually compact, portable, and offer a wide range of features and functionalities.</p><p>System electronic loads are more extensive and integrated into larger test systems or setups. They are designed to handle higher power requirements and are often used in industrial applications or research laboratories.</p><p>Module electronic loads are small, modular units that can be easily integrated into existing test systems or circuit boards. They offer flexibility and scalability for specific testing requirements.</p></p>
