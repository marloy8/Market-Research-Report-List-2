<p><p>Stroboscopy systems refer to devices used for examining vocal cord function and detecting any abnormalities or disorders related to voice production. Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of portable and user-friendly stroboscopy systems, making them more accessible for healthcare professionals and patients.</p><p>Moreover, the rising geriatric population, as well as the growing awareness of voice healthcare among the general population, have contributed to the market growth.
