<p><p>Laboratory ORP (Oxidation-Reduction Potential) Meters are used to measure the electrical potential of a chemical reaction. They are available in three types: Single Channel, Dual Channel, and Multi Channel. </p><p>Single Channel ORP Meters are designed to measure ORP on a single sample at a time, making them suitable for small-scale applications. </p><p>Dual Channel ORP Meters can measure ORP on two samples simultaneously, which is beneficial for comparative analysis or quality control purposes. </p><p>Multi Channel ORP Meters have multiple channels, allowing for the simultaneous measurement of ORP on several samples, making them ideal for high-throughput laboratories or extensive research requirements.</p></p>
