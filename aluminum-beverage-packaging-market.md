<p><p>Aluminum beverage packaging refers to the use of aluminum cans for storing and packaging beverages. There are two types of cans in the market: Draw and Redraw (DRD) cans and Drawn and Wall Ironed (DWI) cans. DRD cans are made by drawing a can body from a flat aluminum sheet and then redrawing it to achieve the desired shape and size. DWI cans, on the other hand, involve drawing and ironing a single cup to create the can body. Both types of cans are widely used in the beverage industry for their convenience, durability, and recyclability.</p></p>
