<p><ul><li>Standard HPLC Pump</li><li>UHPLC Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HPLC pumps market consists of two main types: Standard HPLC pumps and UHPLC pumps. Standard HPLC pumps are commonly used in laboratories for routine analytical applications. They provide reliable performance, ease of use, and compatibility with a wide range of solvent systems. On the other hand, UHPLC pumps are specifically designed for ultra-high-performance liquid chromatography. They offer higher pressure capabilities, faster analysis times, and improved resolution for demanding or complex separations. Both types cater to different needs in the field of liquid chromatography.</p></p>
