<p><p>Gym/Club fitness trackers are categorized into three main types: wrist wear, leg wear, and others. Wrist wear trackers, as the name suggests, are worn on the wrist and often resemble traditional watches. They are lightweight, convenient, and offer features like heart rate monitoring and step counting. Leg wear trackers, on the other hand, are worn on the lower part of the body, such as ankles or shoes, and mainly focus on tracking activities like running or cycling. The "others" category includes fitness trackers that can be worn on different body parts, such as the chest or arm. These trackers cater to specific needs and offer various functionalities suited to different exercise types.</p></p>
