<p><p>The disposable surgical gowns market is segmented into three types based on the material used: PP non-woven material, SMS non-woven material, and others. PP non-woven material gowns are made from polypropylene fibers, providing good breathability and fluid resistance. SMS non-woven material gowns are made from spunbond-meltblown-spunbond fabric, offering better barrier protection against fluids and pathogens. Other types may include materials like polyethylene or breathable film laminates. All these gowns are used in healthcare settings to maintain a sterile environment and protect healthcare workers and patients from infections during surgical procedures.</p></p>
