Light duty positioners are designed for smaller loads and are commonly used in applications such as warehouses. Medium duty positioners are suitable for moderately heavy loads and are commonly used in manufacturing plants. Heavy duty positioners have the highest load capacity and are typically used in industries like construction and mining, where lifting and moving extremely heavy objects are required.</p></p>
