<p><p>The UV Germicidal Lamp market can be categorized into two main types: Straight Tube and H-shape Tube. Straight Tube lamps are cylindrical in shape and emit UV-C light in a straight line. They are commonly used in water treatment, air purification, and surface disinfection applications. On the other hand, H-shape Tube lamps have a U-shaped design, allowing for a wider distribution of UV-C light. They are often utilized in HVAC systems and larger spaces that require comprehensive germicidal coverage. Both types play a crucial role in killing and inactivating harmful microorganisms.</p></p>
