<p><p>The Solenoid Dosing Pump market offers two types of control: Manual Control and Signal Control. In Manual Control, the dosing pump is manually adjusted by the operator to control the dosage rate. This type of control is simple and cost-effective, suitable for applications that do not require precise dosage accuracy. On the other hand, Signal Control allows the dosing pump to be controlled by an external signal, such as a flow meter or a pH sensor. This type enables more precise and automated dosage control, making it ideal for applications that require accurate and consistent dosing.</p></p>
