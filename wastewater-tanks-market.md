<p><p>The wastewater tanks market encompasses three main types: bolted steel tanks, welded steel tanks, and concrete tanks. Bolted steel tanks are constructed by fastening steel panels together with bolts, allowing for flexibility and easier transportation. Welded steel tanks, on the other hand, are created by welding steel sheets together, ensuring a tighter seal but with limited size options. Concrete tanks are made from poured concrete and offer durability and large storage capacity.
