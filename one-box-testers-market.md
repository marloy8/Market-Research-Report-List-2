<p><p>One-Box Testers are categorized into two market types: Single-mode and Double-mode Testers. Single-mode Testers are designed to test optical fibers that transmit only a single mode of light, typically used in long-distance communication networks. Double-mode Testers, on the other hand, are capable of testing optical fibers that transmit multiple modes of light, often used in shorter distance communication networks such as local area networks. Both types of testers play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning and high performance of optical fiber networks.</p></p>
