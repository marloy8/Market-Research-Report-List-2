<p><p>Ready-to-use syringes are widely used in the healthcare industry for drug delivery purposes. The market for ready-to-use syringes can be classified into two types: glass syringes and polymer syringes. Glass syringes are made from high-quality glass and offer enhanced compatibility and sterilization properties. They are commonly used for injecting sensitive drugs. On the other hand, polymer syringes are made from durable plastics and offer advantages such as resistance to breakage and reduced formation of particulate matter. They are suitable for injecting non-sensitive drugs.</p></p>
