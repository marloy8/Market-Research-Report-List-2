<p><p>The POS cash drawer market offers several types of cash drawers to cater to different needs. Printer-driven cash drawers are connected to the receipt printer and open automatically when a transaction is completed. Ethernet-driven cash drawers are connected to the network and can be operated remotely. Serial and USB-driven cash drawers connect to the POS system via serial or USB ports. Lastly, manually-driven cash drawers do not require any electronic connection and need to be opened manually. Each type of cash drawer offers specific features and benefits to suit various business requirements.</p></p>
