<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Speed Industrial Cameras market comprises two major types: Line Scan Camera and Area Scan Camera. Line Scan Cameras are suitable for capturing images of moving objects with high precision. They produce a single line of pixels that captures the object as it moves across the field of view. Area Scan Cameras, on the other hand, capture a 2D image of a stationary object. They are more versatile and commonly used in applications such as inspection, quality control, and surveillance. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in various industrial sectors requiring fast and accurate imaging capabilities.</p></p>
