<p><p>The Hybrid Electric Marine Propulsion Engine market consists of two main types: Electric Power and Blended Fuel. Electric Power engines rely solely on electricity as their primary source of energy, utilizing batteries or fuel cells to power the vessel. On the other hand, Blended Fuel engines combine conventional marine fuels with alternative sources such as liquefied natural gas (LNG) or biofuels, creating a more environmentally-friendly propulsion system. Both types aim to reduce emissions and improve the sustainability of marine transportation.</p></p>
