<p><p>Heat sealing is a process used to join or seal materials together using heat. In the case of PTFE fabrics, there are two main types of heat sealing markets: porous PTFE and non-porous PTFE. Porous PTFE fabrics have tiny holes or pores that allow air and gases to pass through, making them suitable for applications like filtration or ventilation. On the other hand, non-porous PTFE fabrics are solid and impermeable, making them ideal for applications requiring complete barrier protection, such as food packaging or medical devices.</p></p>
