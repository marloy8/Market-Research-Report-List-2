<p><p>Dredging services market types refer to the different methods used to remove sediments and debris from water bodies. Hydraulic dredging involves the use of water and suction to excavate and transport materials, making it suitable for areas with soft sediments. On the other hand, mechanical dredging relies on heavy machinery to physically remove materials, making it more appropriate for areas with hard sediments. These market types cater to various dredging needs and are selected based on the specific characteristics of the project site.</p></p>
