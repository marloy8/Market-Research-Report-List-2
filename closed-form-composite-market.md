<p><p>Closed form composite market types include glass fiber closed molding composites, carbon fiber closed molding composites, natural fiber closed molding composites, and aramid fiber closed molding composites. Glass fiber composites use glass fibers as the reinforcement material, carbon fiber composites use carbon fibers, natural fiber composites use natural fibers like hemp or flax, and aramid fiber composites use aramid fibers such as Kevlar. These composites are fabricated using closed molding techniques, which involve placing the reinforcement material into a mold and then injecting resin to create the final composite product.</p></p>
