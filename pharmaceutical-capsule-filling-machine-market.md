<p><p>The pharmaceutical capsule filling machine market is categorized into three types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual machines. Fully automatic machines are equipped with advanced technology and can operate without human intervention, offering high speed and accuracy in capsule filling. Semi-automatic machines require some level of human involvement, such as loading empty capsules and removing filled ones, providing a balance between efficiency and manual labor. Manual machines rely entirely on human effort, making them suitable for small-scale operations. Each type offers different features and benefits to cater to the specific needs of pharmaceutical companies.</p></p>
