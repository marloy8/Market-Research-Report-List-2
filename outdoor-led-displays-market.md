<p><p>Outdoor LED displays are widely used for advertising and information purposes. There are two main types of LED displays in the market: surface mounted and individually mounted. Surface mounted displays consist of LED modules that are assembled onto a single large board, offering a seamless viewing experience. Individually mounted displays, on the other hand, are composed of discrete LED pixels mounted individually on a screen, providing flexibility for custom designs and easy maintenance. Both types of displays are highly visible and durable, making them suitable for various outdoor applications.</p></p>
