<p><p>The Ebola vaccine market includes various types of vaccines. Inactivated vaccines are created by killing the Ebola virus and injecting it into the body, triggering an immune response. DNA vaccines use genetic material from the virus to stimulate the immune system. Virus vector vaccines use a harmless virus to carry Ebola genes into the body. Other types of vaccines in the market include subunit vaccines, viral protein vaccines, and recombinant protein vaccines. These vaccine types aim to provide effective protection against the Ebola virus and prevent its spread.</p></p>
