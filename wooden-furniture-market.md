<p><p>The wooden furniture market can be divided into three main types: solid wood furniture, wood-based panels furniture, and miscellaneous furniture. Solid wood furniture is made entirely of solid wood and is known for its durability and natural beauty. Wood-based panels furniture, on the other hand, is made by combining solid wood with wood-based panels, such as plywood or particle board, resulting in affordable and versatile furniture. Lastly, the miscellaneous furniture market includes furniture made from other wood materials or composites, offering a wide range of designs and styles to suit different preferences.</p></p>
