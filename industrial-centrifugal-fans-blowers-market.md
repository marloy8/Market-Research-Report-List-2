<p><p>Industrial centrifugal fans & blowers are mechanical devices that are used to increase the airflow within an industrial setting. These fans work based on the principle of centrifugal force, where air enters the fan through the inlet and is pushed outwards by the rotating blades.</p><p>The industrial centrifugal fans & blowers market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. The demand for these fans is being propelled by various factors, including the growth of the HVAC industry, increasing industrialization, and stringent government regulations on energy efficiency.
