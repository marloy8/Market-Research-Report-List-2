<p><strong>The Brass Gate Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solder End</li><li>Threaded End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brass gate valves come in two main market types: solder end and threaded end. Solder end gate valves are designed with a soldering connection, which allows them to be joined to copper pipes through the use of heat and solder. On the other hand, threaded end gate valves possess threads on their ends, enabling them to be easily screwed onto corresponding fittings or pipes. Both types serve the purpose of controlling the flow of fluids or gases and are commonly used in various plumbing and industrial applications.</p></p>
