<p><strong>The RF Feeder System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coax or Coaxial Cable</li><li>Open Wire or Twin Feeder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF Feeder System market includes two main types: coaxial cable and open wire or twin feeder. Coaxial cable is a type of transmission line that consists of two concentric conductors separated by an insulating layer, suitable for transmitting high-frequency signals with low loss. Open wire or twin feeder, on the other hand, is a transmission line that consists of two conductors separated by a gap, typically used in low-frequency applications. Both types serve the purpose of carrying radio frequency signals between the transmitter and antenna in wireless communication systems.</p></p>
