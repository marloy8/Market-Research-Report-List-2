On the other hand, a tray stripping column consists of multiple horizontal trays designed to facilitate mass transfer between the vapor and liquid phases. Both types of columns are used in different applications, such as oil refineries, chemical plants, and distillation processes, to purify or separate mixtures of substances.</p></p>
