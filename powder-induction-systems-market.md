<p><p>Powder induction systems are used for efficient mixing and blending processes. The in-tank mixing market refers to systems where powders are introduced directly into a tank or vessel, allowing for simultaneous mixing. This method is commonly used in industries like food and beverages. On the other hand, the online blending market involves systems that mix powders inline during a production process. This is commonly used in industries such as pharmaceuticals and chemical manufacturing, where precise blending is required. Both markets aim to improve productivity and ensure uniform mixing of powders.</p></p>
