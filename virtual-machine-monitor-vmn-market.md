<p><p>A Virtual Machine Monitor (VMN), also known as a hypervisor, is a software layer that enables the creation and management of multiple virtual machines on a single physical machine. The increasing adoption of cloud computing and virtualization technologies by enterprises across various industries is one of the primary factors driving market growth. The ability of VMNs to consolidate multiple servers onto a single physical machine is attractive to businesses, as it reduces hardware costs and improves overall efficiency.</p><p>Moreover, the rising demand for data center consolidation and server virtualization is also contributing to market growth. VMNs enable businesses to optimize their server infrastructure, reduce power and cooling requirements, and enhance scalability and flexibility.</p><p>Additionally, the rapid growth of digitalization and the internet of things (IoT) is expected to fuel the demand for VMNs.
