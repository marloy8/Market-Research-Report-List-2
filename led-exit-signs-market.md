<p><strong>The LED Exit Signs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrical Exit Signs</li><li>Non-electrical Exit Signs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED Exit Signs market includes two types: Electrical Exit Signs and Non-electrical Exit Signs. Electrical Exit Signs rely on a constant source of electricity to operate, typically sourced from the building's electrical system. They are illuminated by LED lights and often have battery backup systems. Non-electrical Exit Signs, on the other hand, do not require electricity to function. They are typically made of photoluminescent materials that absorb and store ambient light, allowing them to glow in the dark and provide visibility during emergencies. Both types play a vital role in guiding individuals to safety during evacuations.</p></p>
