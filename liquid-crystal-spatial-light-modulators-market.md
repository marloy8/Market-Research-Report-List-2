<p><p>Liquid Crystal Spatial Light Modulators (SLMs) are devices that can alter the amplitude, phase, or polarization of light for various applications. Reflective SLMs use light reflected off their surface and are suitable for applications requiring high optical power. They offer high resolution and efficiency but suffer from limited contrast. Transmissive SLMs, on the other hand, use light transmitted through the device and offer high contrast ratios. They are often used in holographic displays and adaptive optics systems, providing high-quality image reproduction and precise light control.</p></p>
