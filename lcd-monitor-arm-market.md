<p><p>The LCD monitor arm market can be categorized into three types: keyhole, fixture, and wall hanging. Keyhole mounts are designed with key-shaped holes that allow monitors to be easily attached and detached from the arm. Fixture mounts provide a fixed and stable platform for securely holding monitors. Wall hanging mounts, as the name suggests, are designed for mounting the arm on walls, saving desk space and providing a flexible viewing angle. These types offer different installation options to meet varying needs and preferences of consumers in the LCD monitor arm market.</p></p>
