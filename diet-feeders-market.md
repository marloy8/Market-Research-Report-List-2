<p><ul><li>Trailed</li><li>Self Propelled</li><li>Stationary</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diet feeders are agricultural machines used to mix and distribute feed for livestock. There are four main types: Trailed, self-propelled, stationary, and others. "Trailed" diet feeders are towed behind tractors and are suitable for smaller farms. "Self-propelled" diet feeders have their own engine and are suitable for larger farms as they can move independently. "Stationary" diet feeders remain in one place and require animals to come to them for feeding. The category of "Others" includes variations or hybrids of the three main types, or any other type that does not fit into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
