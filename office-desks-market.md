<p><p>Office desks can be categorized into two main market types: fixed and adjustable. Fixed desks are designed with a stationary height and cannot be adjusted. They are typically made using sturdier materials, making them more suitable for heavy-duty use. On the other hand, adjustable desks offer the flexibility to change their height according to the user's preference. They usually have a mechanism that allows for easy adjustment, facilitating ergonomic positioning and promoting comfort during work. The choice between fixed and adjustable desks depends on the user's specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
