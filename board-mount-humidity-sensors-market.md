<p><p>The board mount humidity sensors market consists of two types: analog output and digital output. Analog output sensors provide continuous voltage or current signals proportional to the measured humidity level. They are commonly used in applications where real-time monitoring and control of humidity is necessary. Digital output sensors convert the humidity measurement into digital data, which can be easily transmitted and processed. They offer higher accuracy and reliability, making them suitable for demanding applications such as HVAC systems, medical devices, and industrial automation.</p></p>
