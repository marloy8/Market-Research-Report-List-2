<p><p>The solar thermal collector market consists of different types of collectors. Flat plate collectors are the most common, comprising an insulated metal box with a glass cover and a dark absorber plate to capture sunlight. Evacuated tube collectors consist of multiple glass tubes with a vacuum layer to enhance thermal insulation. Solar air collectors heat air directly, using a collector to absorb sunlight and a fan to circulate air. "Others" represents different collector designs, such as parabolic trough collectors or concentrated solar power systems.</p></p>
