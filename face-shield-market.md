<p><p>The Face Shield market is categorized into two types: Full Face Shield and Half Face Shield. Full Face Shields provide complete coverage for the face, including the eyes, nose, and mouth. They offer enhanced protection against splashes, sprays, and airborne particles. On the other hand, Half Face Shields only cover the lower half of the face, leaving the eyes exposed. They are primarily designed to protect the mouth and nose from droplets and splashes. Both types serve as a physical barrier that helps reduce the risk of infection and ensure safety in various industries and public settings.</p></p>
