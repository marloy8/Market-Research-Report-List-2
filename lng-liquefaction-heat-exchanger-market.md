<p><ul><li>Coiled Heat Exchanger</li><li>Plate - fin Heat Exchanger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LNG liquefaction heat exchanger market includes two types of heat exchangers, namely coiled heat exchangers and plate-fin heat exchangers. Coiled heat exchangers utilize a coiling tube arrangement to transfer heat, making them suitable for small-scale applications. On the other hand, plate-fin heat exchangers use a series of alternating flat plates to achieve efficient heat transfer, making them ideal for large-scale LNG liquefaction processes. Both types play a crucial role in the LNG liquefaction process by facilitating the cooling and condensation of natural gas into its liquid form.</p></p>
