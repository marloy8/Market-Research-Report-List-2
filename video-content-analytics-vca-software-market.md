<p><p>Video Content Analytics (VCA) software market can be classified into two types: camera-based systems and server-based systems. Camera-based systems use analytics capabilities directly on the cameras themselves, allowing for real-time analysis and decision-making at the edge. This minimizes the need for data transfer, reduces network bandwidth, and allows for quicker response times. On the other hand, server-based systems perform video analysis using dedicated servers or cloud-based platforms. These systems enable centralized processing, storage, and analytics capabilities, providing advanced insights and allowing for scalability across multiple cameras or locations.</p></p>
