<p><p>A lung isolation device (LID) is a medical instrument used during thoracic surgeries to collapse and isolate one or more of the patient's lungs. It helps in providing a clear surgical field, making it more convenient for the surgeons to perform the procedure. Lung isolation devices are also used to prevent cross-contamination between the operated and non-operated lungs. These devices are available in various types, including endobronchial blockers, double-lumen endobronchial tubes (DLT), and bronchial blockers.</p><p>The lung isolation device market is experiencing steady growth, and it is expected to continue during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases, such as lung cancer and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), is one of the significant factors driving the market growth. <p><p>The Lung Isolation Device Market consists of two types: Endobronchial Blockers and Lung Dissociation Kits. Endobronchial Blockers are devices used to isolate specific lung lobes during surgical procedures, allowing for selective ventilation. On the other hand, Lung Dissociation Kits are utilized for lung transplantation, as they permit the separation of lung cells and tissues prior to transplantation. Both these devices contribute to improving the efficacy and safety of various lung-related procedures.</p></p>
