<p><p>The Blood Sugar Monitoring Equipment market includes two types: Minimally Invasive and Non-Invasive. Minimally Invasive devices are designed to puncture the skin slightly to extract a small amount of blood for testing glucose levels. On the other hand, Non-Invasive devices measure blood glucose without penetrating the skin, typically using optical sensors or other specialized technologies. These devices are more convenient and less painful for patients, making blood sugar monitoring easier and encouraging regular testing.</p></p>
