<p><p>Infrared Emitters are devices that emit infrared (IR) radiation. There are two common market types of Infrared Emitters: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and SMT (Surface Mount Technology). </p><p>SMD Type Emitters are compact and designed for direct surface mounting onto PCBs (Printed Circuit Boards). They provide efficient heat dissipation and high performance in a small form factor.</p><p>SMT Type Emitters, on the other hand, are designed for automated assembly processes. They have a larger size compared to SMD Type and are typically used in applications where manual soldering is not required.</p><p>Overall, both types of Infrared Emitters cater to different mounting and application requirements in the market.</p></p>
