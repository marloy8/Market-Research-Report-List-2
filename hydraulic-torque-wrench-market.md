<p><p>In the hydraulic torque wrench market, there are two main types: the drive hydraulic wrench and the hollow hydraulic wrench. The drive hydraulic wrench is designed with a square drive that allows it to directly fit onto nuts or bolts for tightening or loosening applications. On the other hand, the hollow hydraulic wrench has a hollow center, allowing it to be used on studs or threaded rods, where the threaded end can pass through the wrench. Both types provide efficient and precise torque application for various industrial purposes.</p></p>
