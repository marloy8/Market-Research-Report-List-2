<p><p>The hard-coated PET film market consists of two types: hard film and foldable film. Hard film refers to a rigid and inflexible form of PET film that provides excellent protection against scratches, UV rays, and other damage. It is commonly used in applications like electronic displays and automotive dashboards. On the other hand, foldable film is a flexible PET film that can be bent or folded without damaging it. It is mostly used in applications like smartphones, tablets, and wearable devices, where flexibility is crucial.</p></p>
