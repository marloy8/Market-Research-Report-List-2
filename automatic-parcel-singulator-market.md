<p><p>The automatic parcel singulator market includes two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. In the semi-automatic market, the singulator requires some human intervention to operate. It typically involves manually loading parcels onto the singulator and pressing buttons to initiate the separating process. On the other hand, the fully automatic market involves singulators that operate without human intervention. These singulators can independently handle parcel separation, loading, and unloading processes, utilizing advanced technologies like sensors and conveyor systems. Both types cater to different customer needs and levels of automation in parcel handling.</p></p>
