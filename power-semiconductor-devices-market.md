<p><p>The power semiconductor devices market includes various types of devices such as Power MOSFETs, IGBTs, Bipolar Power Transistors, Thyristors, and Rectifiers. Power MOSFETs are used for high power applications with low on-resistance. IGBTs integrate characteristics of both MOSFETs and bipolar transistors, suitable for high voltage and high current applications. Bipolar Power Transistors have a bipolar junction and are mainly used in audio amplifiers and power supplies. Thyristors are used for switching applications due to their ability to handle high currents. Rectifiers convert AC to DC power and are used in power supplies and battery chargers.</p></p>
