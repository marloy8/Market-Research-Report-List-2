<p><p>The Medical Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) Systems market consists of two types: Single Phase UPS Systems and Three Phase UPS Systems. Single Phase UPS Systems are designed for smaller medical facilities and use a single phase power source. They provide backup power in case of an outage to critical medical equipment. Three Phase UPS Systems, on the other hand, are suitable for larger healthcare institutions and use three phase power sources. They offer increased power capacity and are capable of supporting more equipment during power interruptions.</p></p>
