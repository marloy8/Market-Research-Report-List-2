<p><p>The drone data services market is categorized into three types: mapping and surveying, photogrammetry, and 3D modeling. Mapping and surveying involve using drones to capture aerial images and collect data for creating accurate maps and conducting land surveys. Photogrammetry utilizes drone imagery to create detailed 3D models of objects or landscapes. 3D modeling, on the other hand, focuses on the creation of virtual representations of physical objects or spaces using drone data. These services provide valuable information for various industries such as construction, agriculture, and urban planning.</p></p>
