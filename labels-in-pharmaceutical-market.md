<p><p>Labels in the pharmaceutical market can be classified into two types: Paper type and Plastic film type labels. Paper type labels are made from paper materials and are commonly used in the packaging of pharmaceutical products. They are cost-effective and provide a traditional appearance. On the other hand, plastic film type labels are made from various types of plastic films, such as polyethylene or polypropylene. These labels offer durability and resistance to moisture, chemicals, and temperature changes. They are commonly used in pharmaceutical products that require higher levels of protection and longevity.</p></p>
