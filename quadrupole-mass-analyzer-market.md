<p><ul><li>Single Quadrupole Mass Analyzer</li><li>Triple Quadrupole Mass Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quadrupole Mass Analyzer market can be divided into two main types: Single Quadrupole Mass Analyzer and Triple Quadrupole Mass Analyzer. </p><p>A Single Quadrupole Mass Analyzer is a type of mass spectrometer that uses a single set of quadrupole rods to selectively separate and analyze ions based on their mass-to-charge ratio. It is commonly used for qualitative and quantitative analysis, making it suitable for applications such as environmental monitoring, forensic analysis, and pharmaceutical research.</p><p>On the other hand, a Triple Quadrupole Mass Analyzer consists of two sets of quadrupole rods in series, with a collision cell in between. This configuration provides enhanced capabilities for precise quantification and structural elucidation of analytes. Triple Quadrupole Mass Analyzers are extensively used in fields like drug discovery, food safety, and metabolomics, where high sensitivity and selectivity are required for accurate measurements.</p></p>
