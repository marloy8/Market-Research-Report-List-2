<p><p>An External Drainage and Monitoring System refers to a medical device used for the treatment and management of conditions such as hydrocephalus or excessive accumulation of cerebrospinal fluid in the brain. The system includes a catheter and a valve to drain excess fluid from the brain to another part of the body, typically the abdomen, where it can be safely absorbed and eliminated by the body.
