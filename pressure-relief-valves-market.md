<p><p>Pressure relief valves are essential safety devices used in various industries to protect equipment, pipelines, and systems from excessive pressure buildup. There are three main types of pressure relief valves available in the market. </p><p>Direct acting pressure relief valves directly respond to pressure changes and automatically open to release excess pressure. </p><p>Pilot operated pressure relief valves utilize a separate pressure control mechanism to open and close the main valve. This design allows for accurate pressure control and handling of higher pressures.</p><p>Other types of pressure relief valves include balanced bellows valves, thermal relief valves, and safety valves. These valves offer specific features and are chosen based on the requirements of the system in which they are installed.</p></p>
