<p><p>Automated Optometry Equipment refers to advanced technology used in eye care that reduces manual intervention and enhances accuracy. Fixed Automatic Optometry Equipment includes devices like auto refractor keratometers, non-contact tonometers, and automated lensometers, which are permanently installed in clinics or hospitals. On the other hand, Portable Automatic Optometry Equipment includes handheld autorefractors, portable tonometers, and compact lensometers that offer flexibility in terms of mobility and can be used in remote locations or for regular home eye exams. Both types of equipment aim to streamline eye examinations and improve patient experience.</p></p>
